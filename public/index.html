UNITRANS 2020-2021 SERVICE CHANGE PROPOSALS

Unitrans is proposing the following changes to bus service to take effect in August 3, 2020 for the 2020-21 academic year. Proposals focus on improving your ride by making it faster and more reliable. For more information on each proposal, go to unitrans.ucdavis.edu. To comment, email comments@unitrans.ucdavis.edu, call 530-752-2877, or mail Unitrans, 1 Shields Avenue, 5 South Hall, Davis, CA 95616. The comment period will close on Friday, May 22, 2020.

RELIABILITY IMPROVEMENTS

All Door Boarding Pilot

  • Customers with passes will be allowed to board through any door to decrease boarding times. Only cash payers and 10-ride ticket holders will be required to board through the front doors. We trust our valued customers to continue to pay for their trips. As a reminder, Unitrans already entrusts customers at the Silo and Memorial Union Bus Terminals to pay their fare. Unitrans is not free at terminals. Pilot will last for up to a year.

Morning Schedule Update on All Lines

  • All morning schedules will shift five minutes earlier between 6:30 AM and 9:00 AM to provide extra time for 9:00 AM arrivals. No changes to campus departures after 9:00 AM.
  • Sample: P Line Departures from the Memorial Union Terminal

Drop Off Only Stop Pilot on the G, J, and V Lines:

  • To improve travel times, Unitrans proposes drop off only stops at outbound (away from campus) bus stops on Alvarado Avenue and Sycamore Lane for the G/J lines and on Hutchison Drive at Sage Street and Sage Street at West Village Square on the V line. Customers will not be allowed to board at drop off only stops.

Afternoon Schedule Update on V Line

  • Unitrans proposes adjusting the 25 minute after the hour afternoon and evening Silo Terminal V Line departures to 20 minutes after the hour to better meet customer demand.

CAPACITY FOCUSED IMPROVEMENTS

Unitrans proposes:

  • Increased weekday morning capacity trips on the A and V lines
  • New 7:10 PM Z line and 8:10 PM K and Z line trips
  • Elimination of 6:20 PM D, G, J, and W lines and 6:25 PM V line trips. Buses will be used on overcrowded and late 6:35 PM trips

REMINDER FROM 2019

A/O/Z Line Westbound Reroute Near the Davis Amtrak Station

  • As approved in April 2019, Unitrans will implement the approved A/O/Z line westbound reroute near the Davis Amtrak Station if safety incidents increase.
